About Us

Center Research Technology Development abbreviated as CRTD RESEARCH CENTER aims to provide quality education to all children at elementary level. Build a society committed to constitutional values with the help of quality education and literacy. In order to make the dream of education a reality for every deserving student in the country, an initiative by government is taken to ensure the upliftment of every section of the society. Bring equity to the system of secondary education by including disadvantaged groups as well as weaker sections. Ensure equality and improved standards of education by supporting the existing institutions and facilitating, setting up of new ones.
